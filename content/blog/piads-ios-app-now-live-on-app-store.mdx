We built PiAds to make local advertising simple. Today it gets even simpler.

**The PiAds iOS app is now live on the App Store.** [Download it here.](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/piads/id6759892788)

Whether you're a venue owner managing screens or a local business booking ads, everything you need is now in your pocket.

## What You Can Do

### For Advertisers

The app gives you the full marketplace experience on mobile:

- **Browse local venues** — See every screen in your area on a map. Filter by venue type (gym, cafe, coworking, restaurant), audience, and pricing.
- **Book campaigns** — Pick your screens, choose your dayparts (breakfast, lunch, evening), upload your creative, and book — all from your phone.
- **Track performance** — See how many plays your ads have delivered, check debt status, and monitor active campaigns in real time.
- **Manage creatives** — Upload images and videos directly from your camera roll. Review and swap creatives without touching a computer.
- **Cart & checkout** — Add multiple screens to your cart and check out with Stripe in a few taps.

### For Venue Owners

If you run a venue with PiAds screens, the app puts your CMS in your pocket:

- **Manage screens** — See all your screens, their status, and what's currently playing.
- **Edit playlists** — Add, remove, or reorder content on your playlists.
- **View schedules** — Check what's running during each daypart.
- **Upload media** — Snap a photo or shoot a quick video and push it to your screens instantly.
- **Switch workspaces** — Toggle between your Advertiser and Venue dashboards with one tap.

## Why Mobile Matters

Most of our venue owners aren't sitting at desks. They're behind counters, on gym floors, running between meetings. When something needs to change on a screen — a new promotion, an updated class schedule, a holiday announcement — they need to do it right then, not "when I get back to my laptop."

Same for advertisers. A local business owner spots a venue they want to advertise in while grabbing coffee. Now they can book that screen before they finish their latte.

## What's Inside

The app is built natively for iOS with the same features as the web dashboard:

- **Discover tab** — Interactive map of all available screens with venue details, audience info, and pricing
- **Campaigns tab** — Create, monitor, and manage your ad campaigns
- **Cart** — Multi-screen booking with Stripe checkout
- **Creatives** — Upload and manage your ad media assets
- **Settings** — Switch between Advertiser and Venue workspaces, manage your profile, notifications, and venues

## Get Started

1. [Download PiAds from the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/piads/id6759892788)
2. Sign in with your existing account (or create one in 2 minutes)
3. Start browsing venues or managing your screens

If you're already a PiAds user, your account, screens, campaigns, and everything else will be right there when you log in.
