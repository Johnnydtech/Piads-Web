What if the screen on your vending machine could pay for itself?

That's exactly what's happening with [TheLuxScents](https://www.theluxscents.com) — a luxury fragrance vending machine company based in Arlington, VA. Their machines sit in hotel lobbies, luxury apartments, and upscale venues, dispensing curated niche fragrances to guests with high-end taste. Each machine comes equipped with an HD touchscreen display.

And those screens? They're running PiAds.

## The Setup: Fragrance Machine Meets Digital Signage

Every TheLuxScents machine — from the 12-slot Classic to the 36-slot Elite — features an HD display that customers interact with while browsing fragrances. They see product details, brand stories, and scent profiles on screen as they shop.

But here's the thing: customers aren't browsing 24/7. Between interactions, those screens used to sit idle — showing a logo or a screensaver.

Now they show premium advertisements from vetted luxury brands. High-end fashion, premium skincare, luxury travel, fine dining — the kind of ads that feel like a natural extension of the space, not an interruption.

PiAds makes this happen automatically.

## Two Revenue Streams, Zero Extra Work

This is the part venue operators love. With PiAds integrated into every TheLuxScents machine, there are now two distinct revenue streams:

1. **Fragrance sales** — customers buy perfumes directly from the machine
2. **Ad impressions** — brands pay to reach the audience standing right in front of the screen

The second stream is completely passive. PiAds handles ad sales, content management, scheduling, and reporting. The venue operator doesn't have to do anything. The machine earns money whether someone buys a fragrance or not.
